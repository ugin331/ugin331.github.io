DeftSign

The Deftsign Translator Glove is a project I have been working on for quite some time. The basic concept is a glove with sensors in the fingers, in order to help translate the user's finger/hand movements into audible sounds. The glove is intended to help users with Speech Impediments say the things they simply cannot say, or have significant trouble saying. For example, if a hypothetical user had trouble saying certain words invovling buying items at a store, they could bind various hand signs to the words/phrases that they had trouble saying to make the process of purchasing items much simpler and less awkward. The glove also features Machine Learning predictive technology to help eliminate mistranslations based on the context of previous phrases used, and features a two-part design that allows the user to wash the device while preserving the electronics, keeping costs low.
